Nexo Price Analysis: NEXO Trades at Red Zone of Volatility, Wait for Weekly Closing

in Cryptocurrency, Markets, News
0

  • Nexo token started making lower-highs formation in terms of the 4-hours price scale. 
  • The 20 and 50-day moving averages are increasing below the current price of Nexo. 
  • Despite selling, bulls barely maintain a conceptual support level of $1.0.

NEXO token moves on a bullish path for the past several days. Altcoin is outperforming after reversing from its recent bottom level. As a result, buyers celebrated over 110% gain in the last two months from $0.54 to $1.19 level. During higher momentum, buyers became the witness of roller-coaster rides. 

NEXO on 4-Hours Chart 

In the 4-hours price scale, bulls continue buying bitcoin in each retest of the upwards trendline. After recording a weekly high, and as per the Nexo review started moving lower with lower-highs formation because bears have set their order at 20-DMA (on the weekly price chart).

During the sell-off, bulls barely maintained a conceptual support level of $1.0 for the past several days. Meanwhile, the token is trading at $1.03 mark at the time of writing. Price may slip towards the horizontal support trendline before another rise. Thus, the pair price of NEXO token along with the Bitcoin pair is down 3.3% at 0.0000518 Satoshis. 

Bulls Have a Key Hedge Zone of $1.0 Mark

The red alert will execute if buyers fail to maintain a conceptual round level of $1.0; below this support, buyers may become aggressive at $0.90 level. The trading volume seems to be neutral at $27 Million as per the last 24-hours data of CMC.

The significant moving averages like 20,50 and 100 are below the current price of NEXO. Besides, RSI got rejection at the 70-level, suggesting a slightly negative indication for the short-term view in the context of daily price scale. 

Conclusion 

NEXO token seems to be sideways for the past four trading sessions. However, price action shows bearish formation in a lower time frame, which may drag the price lower towards the upwards trendline. 

Resistance level– $1.4 and $2.0

Support level– $1.0 and $0.55
