As the cryptocurrency market keeps growing, crypto investors, enthusiasts, and traders need to keep up with what is happening around the market almost in real time. The surest way to achieve this is by keeping tabs with cryptocurrency news.

But therere hundreds of cryptocurrency activities being reported in a 24-hour period; from regulations, scams, investment opportunities, new crypto coins etc. To help filter through, cryptocurrency news aggregators have come up. These are websites or platforms that list the headlines of whats happening in the crypto world plus links to the actual news articles on third-party websites.

Below we are going to look at 14 of the best cryptocurrency news aggregators.

1. CryptoPanic

Simply the best aggregator weve found in the market is CryptoPanic. On one screen, visitors can see news from all over the cryptosphere and just how new that news is.

On CryptoPanic, readers can sort news in categories like bullish or rising to see positive news from coins and crypto projects. Users can also sign-up and fill out their portfolio to sort news by coins theyre hodlingor projects theyre interested in.

Cryptopanic has many more features including a powerful search bar, a feature on polls, and a sidebar which includes all major coin prices and movements.

We check CryptoPanic daily to understand whats happening in this ecosystem. Any serious crypto investor has CryptoPanic bookmarked and returns back at least a few times each day to see whats happening.

2. CryptoGoat

This website provides an easy and clean platform for those seeking to keep abreast with whats happening in the cryptocurrency and blockchain circles. To ensure only quality and trustworthy news reach the user, CryptoGoat features only the top publishers. They include AMBcrypto, blokt, chepicap, CoinDesk, NewsBTC, Zycrypto, Finance Magnates, among other leading cryptocurrency/blockchain news sites.

The platform does not feature news feeds from social media platforms like Reddit or Twitter. For them, this would be duplication because they believe, social media platforms act as news aggregators.

Additionally, CryptoGoat does not offer an opportunity to filter the news depending on e.g coin, or category etc. Having the news sources mixed is the best way to help the user to have a general overview of what is going on in the cryptosphere.

To have an unbiased position while aggregating the news, CryptoGoat does not provide any recommendations on any featured news site and it does not provide its own content whether free or paid.

3. Coinna

This is another great crypto news aggregator platform. It gathers news pieces from established virtual currency news sources and displays the links at a central point.  A few of the places the platform gets its news stories includes Bitsonline, CoinCodex, Finance Magnates, among other top publishers.

To save the reader the hustle of grabbing the latest news, the platform has a black and white theme. Among the top features of Coinna is the default News section that displays the news as they stream in. Old posts are pushed down the queue with new posts occupying the top position.

It also features filters which help the reader choose between topics like Bitcoin, blockchain, crypto mining, individual coins, etc.

The website has a section dedicated to trading or other cryptocurrency/ blockchain aspect packaged in the form of tutorials. However, articles on this section come from reputable crypto/blockchain websites.

Coinna has a Coinbase-only section which holds anything and everything related to Coinbase, a leading crypto exchange and wallet provided in the United States.

Another notable section on this website is one that aggregates information regarding initial coin offerings and startups preparing or have recently introduced a service or product in the crypto world.

4. FAWS

This is a unique cryptocurrency news aggregator. More that gathering what is happening around cryptocurrencies, it also provides a coin price alert, portfolios, and a coin price check.

This makes it a one-stop-platform for everything cryptocurrency. Apart from CoinDesk, CoinTelegraph, and Bitcoin.com crypto websites, FAWS sources its news from 37 other top sources.

On visiting the platform, you will first meet the news page. From the page, you can search whatever you want to keep tabs on. This can range from individual cryptocurrencies to press releases. For the coins section, FAWs aggregates coin prices from top cryptocurrency exchanges like Gemini and Poloniex. It also draws its information from CoinMarketCap, the leading crypto prices aggregator platform.

FAWS understands that a portfolio includes different digital coins, as such, it has the portfolio section where users can bring in all their assets and the platform gives them an estimate of how much they are worth in terms of their held digital wealth.

5. CoinSpectator

Here, investors and traders are welcome to keep tabs of both crypto prices and news. The platform fetches its information from other high flying cryptocurrency websites.

For the cryptocurrency prices, CoinSpectator only features price of the top sixteen coins based on their market capitalization.

Additionally, the website gives information on crypto projects that will be launching in the near future. Unlike other news aggregator platforms, CoinSpeaker features news feed from social media platforms e.g Twiter. It also features information on ICOs whether currently running or soon to be launched.

To help keep you updated on the go, the platform gives you the option to receive an updated list of whats happening directly in your inbox.

6. Ccowl

Ccowl is a well laid out website to help the reader easily navigate from crypto news to prices and back. For the news, the platform fetches its news from only the best crypto news website.  For example, on Ccowl, you will find news from CoinDesk, NewsBTC, CCN, Bitcoin.com among others.

The website provides its own cryptocurrency index that helps to give an overview of the combined market capitalization of 50 cryptocurrencies. However, to give an honest over, the index does not feature the top cryptocurrency, Bitcoin.  This helps the reader to have an unbiased knowledge of how the cryptocurrency market is performing at any given time, at a glance.

The platform has a rank change alert that shows whether a cryptocurrency has changed its rank. Here, twenty-five cryptocurrencies are monitored.  The prices of various cryptocurrencies being monitored on the platform come from Kraken, HitBTC, Binance, Bittrex, Poloniex, and Bitfinex which are all respected cryptocurrency exchanges.

For the overall market capitalization, Ccowl uses CoinMarketCap to fetch the supply of all coins listed and multiplying it by the rates offered by the exchanges.

7. CryptoCoin

This is another reputable crypto news aggregator which has been in existence since late 2014. The website offers news updates from various cryptocurrency news platforms. It also caters for those interested in the technology that underpins cryptocurrencies, Blockchain.

CryptoCoin offers search capabilities and a section for the initial coin offering currently open to investors plus the new coins in the market. For the news section, the platform allows the user to filter using parameters such as a particular coin, year, month, etc.

Unlike other platforms that we have discussed so far, CryptoCoin has a dedicated section for crypto and blockchain-related e-books plus a download option.

To keep users engaged, it features a Did You Know? section where it gives interesting but informative facts about cryptocurrencies. Users can also ask questions or find answers to their queries on the Frequently Asked Question (FAQ) section.

To further drive user engagement, CryptoCoin lets users suggest a virtual currency to be added on the platform.

8. CoinLive

The platform flaunts itself as the go-to website when searching for an aggregated list of real-time cryptocurrency market analysis and news. From its website, CoinLive is deeply invested in providing more than virtual currency news. The cryptocurrency news section is easy to use. It includes news from top crypto news platforms and includes ICOs which have recently launched or plan to launch in the near future.

On the platform, you will find video interviews and detailed and professional market analysis and surveys. The platform welcomes contributions from traders in the form of insights, analysis or theses. This is done by joining the platforms crowdsourcing movement.

With such an approach, the platform seeks to drive cryptocurrency adoption by providing news, market analysis and contributions from traders who have experienced crypto first hand. In doing so, an individual who is testing the cryptocurrency waters gets to understand the market better.

9. CoinLib

More than just fresh digital currency news, CoinLib provides alerts, charts, individual coin data, and a portfolio. All these are fetched and displayed in real-time and is accessible free of charge.

CoinLib sets itself apart from the other cryptocurrency news aggregator sites by allowing a user to put together a portfolio which they can track. The portfolio can also include a cryptocurrency that you do not hold but you are interested in knowing how it performs among its counterparts.

The website provides a section where a user can have a glance of the ranks occupied by different cryptocurrencies and is alerted of a change in case one cryptocurrency unseats another from its position. Additionally, CoinLib allows a user to activate the night mode which kicks in a dark theme enhancing the visibility of the sites contents.

With CoinLib you can select an individual cryptocurrency and scout for the best price on the market. This is facilitated by the Best Price Explorer tool embedded in the website.

10. Flipboard

This website was born in 2018 and offers a mixture of cryptocurrency news and other technology related news. Flipboard assumes a unique approach in that it features podcasts, breakthroughs, opinions, experts take and a host of other financial related news.

Flipboard also gives recommendations on books in various fields. Also, with Flipboard, there is an exclusive section where your teammates can access and share content and acts. The platform is accessible both on desktop and mobile phones. However, for a better view of the cryptocurrency news which falls under the technology section, the desktop version of the platform is the best.

If you do not want to constantly be on the platform for the latest crypto news, but you still need to be updated, Flipboard allows users to sign-up and daily news updates are delivered to their inbox. Instead of general news, the platform allows the user to choose the type of news they want delivered in their inbox on a daily basis.

11. Crypto News

This is another great news aggregator platform. Crypto News focusses on crypto mining, initial coin offering, blockchain, and general cryptocurrency news. The platform is packaged in the form of an Android application. This implies that the platform is only accessible from Android-powered mobile devices.

Among its top sources of cryptocurrency news is Forklog, CoinDesk, Bit Media, and CoinTelegraph. The platform also adds other sources from time to time.

Crypto News provides cryptocurrency news related to market analytics, predictions and opinions; the different exchange rates offered by a select number of cryptocurrency exchanges; predictions into the future of the distributed ledger technology; cryptocurrency trading perspectives; the latest trends in cryptocurrency mining activities, plus news around digital currency mining and the state of initial coin offerings.

You can download the Crypto News Android App and Crypto News iOS App 

12. Algory

This closes our list of the 11 top crypto news aggregators. However, do not mistake it being last to it being inferior among news aggregators.

The platform was built in 2018 with the day to day crypto trader in mind. Algory flaunts itself as the largest crypto news aggregator in the market to date. Apart from aggregating news from top cryptocurrency news websites, Algory also collects crypto information from companies offering cryptocurrency and or blockchain related products or services.

Moreover, it fetches virtual currency-related data from Twitter, Reddit, and other social media platforms. However, data collected on these platforms is analyzed and only added to the feed if it affects the overall crypto price fluctuations.

During its launch in August last year, Algory indicated that it will provide crypto/blockchain news from close to 1000 sources.

The platform contains filters which help the user to make use of the platform within the shortest time possible by accessing only what they are interested in. Even with such a large number of news sources, the platform does not compromise on speed. One of its unique features is allowing users to create dedicated workspaces and news on airdrops and locks in deposit/withdrawals.

13. Coinjoy

Coinjoy is a cryptocurrency and blockchain news aggregator that is providing information about the market and the daily news that take place in the industry. The page has already added support to the largest and most popular cryptocurrency news sites in the world, including UseTheBitcoin.

The site has a dark interface that makes it very comfortable for our eyes to see and follow the latest news. At the same time, Coinjoy has a menu with three different sections: Sources, News and Media. In the Sources page, you can select and unselect the sites from which you want to receive updates and news. If you unselect one of these sites, you will not receive more information and news published in it.

Furthermore, the site is also sharing with the community live prices of virtual currencies on the right side of the screen. This is going to make it very easy for users to have the latest information about the crypto market and whether the latest news are having a positive, neutral or negative impact on Bitcoin (BTC) and other virtual currencies.

In the future, Coinjoy could add new features and information about the crypto market, sites and many other things.

14. Coin News

Coin News is one of the best cryptocurrency news aggregators. It collects news around the world in 6 languages and supports all available platforms: Website, IOS, and Android applications.

This service is different from other applications by its features. It sends push notifications with new articles as soon as the article appears, also it has an email newsletter with the best news of the day or the week, powerful search, offline reading, and more.

Coin News will be a good choice for those who like to receive important information as soon as it appears.

Conclusion

From our discussion above, you can comfortably keep yourself updated on the latest news from the cryptocurrency market. This ranges from general news to specific information affecting cryptocurrency exchanges or companies or individual cryptocurrency projects.

Our above list of the top crypto news aggregators ranges from simple platforms to sophisticated platforms which offer more than news. These platforms feature things like resourceful video interviews, crypto/blockchain e-books, portfolio, and the best crypto exchange rates and guides.

Keep checking this article for updates as new aggregators come. Well be sure to notify you of any great ones that you should be reading.
