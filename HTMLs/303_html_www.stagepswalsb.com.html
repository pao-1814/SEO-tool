About us

Founded in 1924, the Academy of Legal Studies in Business (International) is an association of professors and other scholars in the fields of business law, legal environment, and law-related courses primarily outside of professional law schools. Our nearly 1,000 members teach predominately in schools of business in colleges and universities, at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. Members come from all fifty states, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, England, and a number of other nations. The Pacific Southwest Academy of Legal Studies in Business is one of eight regional subdivisions of the Academy of Legal Studies in Business (ALSB).


The academy provides a forum for the exchange of ideas, and encourages support and cooperation among those who teach and conduct research in the field of legal studies. In addition, the ALSB works to foster understanding and cooperation between college and university administrators and our constituency.
