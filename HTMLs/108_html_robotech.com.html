Normally, when an Anime is to be dubbed/subbed in English for an oficial North American release, the credits tend to mention not only the Region 1 DVD release date, but also the date of the Anime’s original Japanese release. Anyway, while I watching the ADV Films volumes of “Super Dimension Fortress Macross”, I noticed two DVD credit errors: 1) no mentioning of 1982 (the original Japanese release date for this series) while 1985 (release date of “Robotech”) and 2006 (release date for the show’s ADV Films volumes) are mentioned, and 2) Big West wasn’t mentioned for the credit of trademark ownership while it happens to own the entire “Macross” franchise and holds partial production right to each installment of it.

Sounds insane to you?

Tatsunoko Productions, not Big West, owns production/distribution rights to the series, and it is from them that HG purchased Macross.

Big West owns creative rights to the franchise (and production rights to all derivative series/movies they produced, or so I would think), but that is sort of after the fact as far as the original 1982 series is concerned.

1985 and 2006 would be the correct dates for an HG copywright listing in the titles.  I find the absence of a 1982 copywright for TP to be rather surprising though.  I would think that info should come first in the credits (ie, “Copyright 1982 Tatsunoko Productions, LTD; 1985, 2006 Harmony Gold, USA”, etc).

Mentioning Big West seems sort of optional, since as I said above the existance of the franchise is sort of after the fact.  But from a cynical perspective, I agree it’s not hard to imagine HG intentionally leaving all mention of them out if they felt so inclined.
