An ichimoku cloud strategy is a simple way to identify trend and price movement. This strategy combines various technical indicators. These include the Conversion Line and Base Line. When they cross, the trend is more likely to be bullish or bearish. These indicators also work as support and resistance levels. The Ichimoku Cloud can be used to identify trends on its own, or can be used in conjunction with other indicators.
