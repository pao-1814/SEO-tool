INFINITE's Dongwoo says he was scammed out of money 6 times?

AKP STAFF

INFINITE's Dongwoo says he's learned not to get scammed out of money. 


On the January 10th installment of 'Weekly Idol', INFINITE featured as guests to promote their new track "Tell Me", and Dongwoo happily revealed he doesn't fall for scams anymore. He previously shared, "I've been scammed by car sales 6 times. I've been researching a lot lately, so I don't get scammed. As expected, a person needs to study things."

However, Defconn and Jung Hyung Don then found out Dongwoo was very much into cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, and Jung Hyung Don stated, "I heard you're the only one who pays rent monthly [instead of yearly]." He also told viewers, "Please avoid Dongwoo if you see him on the streets."
