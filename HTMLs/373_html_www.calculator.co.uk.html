Is Crypto Lending Beneficial and What Platforms are Best Suited to it?

Is Crypto Lending Beneficial and What Platforms are Best Suited to it?

Posted By: Sam
September 14, 2022

Investment

Print | Email

There have been controversial discussions and advice about cryptocurrency lending in the crypto market. A lot of traders, investors, buyers, and sellers of cryptocurrency lack adequate information, and guidance about crypto lending. However, this article is here to discuss in-depth crypto lending, if it is beneficial, platforms you can lend from including sites like Nexo and its exclusive Nexo interest rates.

What is Crypto Lending?

Cryptocurrency lending is a process whereby investors lend out cryptocurrencies to borrowers in return for interest payments. Note that crypto lending is not done solely by investors, but there are companies and platforms which start up the crypto business, protect it with commercial crypto insurance and lend out to emerging and exciting prospects for entrepreneurs.

With crypto lending, there is the possibility of earning more interest on crypto assets without trading or selling them to investors. Similar to the way traditional banks lend out physical currency and lenders get to earn interest, such applies to how crypto lending works. Payments can be made in whichever form of cryptocurrency is deposited either on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.

Crypto lending isn’t just for borrowers but also for lenders as they both get their benefits. The crypto lending platforms grew more massive in 2020 and have since then been established to expand into billions on different platforms. As earlier stated, the two main features of crypto lending are the efficient loaning of cryptocurrencies and the ability of depositors to gain interest. When users deposit their cryptocurrency, the lending platform requires interest payments on borrowed cryptocurrencies based on the type of cryptocurrency and the platform.

How Does Crypto Lending Work?

The loan terms can be as short as a week or group as four months and charge an hourly interest rate, like Binance. However, other lending platforms offer an indefinite line of credit such as Nexo. Nexo offers 0% APR [Annual Interest Rate]. An indefinite line of credit here refers to how borrowers can take out the money as needed until a particular limit is reached. As the crypto is returned, it can be borrowed again in the case of an open line of credit. Nexo has been proven to be a solid platform and operates in a standard firm as far as cryptocurrency lending is concerned. Nexo interest rates are available for both cryptocurrencies and fiat currencies at the point of lending.

Also, they offer a higher interest percentage for investors to keep a percentage of Nexo in their portfolios. Nexo approves over 20 cryptocurrencies such as Ethereum, Cardano, Dogecoin, Binance, Binance coin, Chainlink, Bitcoin cash, and many more. Nexo’s interest rate system offers 10% starting on stablecoins, and 6% for altcoins. There is also room to earn up to 12% in pounds, euros, and US dollars on annual interest. If you decide to make use of Nexo, there will be 12% off your rewards. On Nexo, interest is calculated daily and you get to withdraw your interest or deposit at your disposal. One exciting thing which suits every crypto lender is no minimum balance is set up to make use of the platform. You can also come back to borrow and also invest in crypto.

Advantages of Crypto Lending

Low-interest rates: Due to their high collateral requirements which make them much more secure for the lender, cryptocurrency loans typically have lower interest rates than those offered by traditional banks We wouldn’t recommend using crypto loans for significant purchases because they are still more expensive than the rates provided by the majority of mortgage or auto loan programs.

Ii. Available platforms to manage loans: A borrower would not need to find lenders for cryptocurrency. There are smart contracts that control the whole process, which makes lending and borrowing easier, more efficient, and safe.

Disadvantages of Crypto Lending

I.) You may not be able to access your funds quickly as you would like on some loan services. This lack of liquidity can jeopardize your financial stability, particularly if too much of your capital is invested in loans and so immobile.

ii.) A margin call will be made by your lender if the value of your collateral decreases. Then, by a particular date, you must deposit further collateral. In the absence of payment, the lender will send your collateral. You will suffer a loss in this scenario, but you keep the borrowed funds.

iii.) Lastly, you will need to deposit some cryptocurrencies to take advantage of any lending opportunities, which exposes you to the risk of price swings eroding your collateral. If you are careless, the reward may be significant and not be worth the effort.
