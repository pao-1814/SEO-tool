How to Choose a Bitcoin Wallet?

By
Diana Conley
-
August 4, 2021

    On average, there are about 100 million Bitcoin owners and 200 million Bitcoin wallets. Plus, around 400,000 Bitcoin users take part in daily trading. Going by these figures, it becomes apparent that you stand to gain a lot from a Bitcoin wallet. 

    Before owning it, you need to choose the kind of bitcoin wallet you wish to have. It is an important step that can make or break the deal for your investments in funds.

    Once you decide on a Bitcoin wallet, filling it up with Bitcoins wouldn’t be a big deal. There are several cryptocurrency loan platforms from which you can get a crypto loan involving Bitcoins.

    No matter whichever Bitcoin wallet you choose, your objective should be to make the right decision. For this to happen, you must bear a few things in mind. Read on to know all that you need to do to pick up the right Bitcoin wallet for your investment needs.

    Top Considerations to Choose the Best Bitcoin Wallet

    You need to remember the top factors to choose a Bitcoin wallet. These include the following:

    1. Pay attention to security features

    When it comes to a web wallet, there is no substitute for security. Encryption features characterized by HTTPS, strong login credentials, and two-factor authentication are some prominent security features of web wallets. Don’t forget to check these features to verify that a given wallet is secure.

    1. Check if a platform has the multi-signature feature

    Some Bitcoin platforms necessitate users to sign in with two different sets of credentials for the completion of a transaction. Known as multisignature (multisig wallet), this feature resembles a lock box in which users from both parties need to sign in with two different sets of keys to open it. 

    This process helps secure Bitcoins from the actions of cybercriminals. It helps prevent cyber theft.

    1. Find out if a platform you are choosing would allow you to exercise control over your Bitcoin credentials

    These days, merely owning a Bitcoin wallet amounts to nothing. You need a wallet with which you can exercise total control over the Bitcoins you own. Besides, it should allow you to back up your data beyond the scope of the services offered by your chosen platform. 

    All of the above is possible only if you get private keys associated with your Bitcoin wallet. Ensure the availability of this feature before finalizing your decision.

    1. Opt for a hierarchical deterministic wallet

    Some wallets allow users to change Bitcoin addresses. By this feature, they provide an additional layer of privacy. When it comes to using wallets, adding new addresses to them enhances their privacy feature for the benefit of users. 

    To make certain that you get a wallet with this feature, check if the one you are choosing is a hierarchical deterministic wallet.

    1. Attach importance to transparency

    While all Bitcoin wallet providers claim their products to be the best for users, they have their share of vulnerabilities. As a user, you need to invest in a Bitcoin wallet that has the least number of vulnerabilities. You can do it by focusing on the transparency of each individual wallet. 

    Transparent providers do not conceal details concerning their identity or operations or the information about their wallet being open source. Before deciding on a provider, check if it is willing to share all these details. This will give you an indication of the transparency of a provider.

    1. Check if a platform is anonymous

    Most users of Bitcoin wallets value their anonymity. Therefore, they look for wallets with minimum registration requirements. While some providers have minimum registration requirements of just an email address, others necessitate KYC verification. 

    Choose a platform depending on whichever option of anonymity suits you in the best possible way.

    1. Assess if a wallet is easy to use

    Without any doubt, everyone wants to use technology. But things can be tricky if one needs to learn the technology behind something before using it. Because a Bitcoin wallet also involves technology, you cannot rule out this possibility. 

    Whether it is about usage or storage, it would be in your best interest to go for a wallet that comes with easy-to-use features. This way, you wouldn’t need to scratch your head to figure out how to use your Bitcoin wallet. 

    Check if your wallet allows you to download Bitcoins to your mobile device or any other compatible device, or you need to store your Bitcoins in a hardware component so that you can access them without the Internet.

    1. Does your chosen platform allow you to back up your data?

    Before filling your wallet with Bitcoins, you need to consider a few points. Firstly, find out if your Bitcoin wallet is encrypted. 

    The next thing that you need to know is if your chosen platform would allow you to back up data concerning your Bitcoins and their transactions. This is a must-have feature in an authentic Bitcoin wallet. 

    1. Review the track record of a wallet to find out if it is reputed

    Reputed Bitcoin wallets come with brand value. As such, you can always bank on them to meet your expectations. Reputed Bitcoin wallets would have higher ratings and lesser negative feedback from users.

    To know if a Bitcoin wallet is reputed or not, check out its online reviews. You can form an opinion of its brand value by going through the reviews of its previous users. Also, do not forget to check out what peers have to say about it.

    Final thoughts

    Bitcoin is a good payment system. Owning a Bitcoin wallet with some Bitcoins in it will help you switch to the modern method of payment. If you are planning to get a 

    Bitcoin wallet to reap its benefits these days, bear the above tips in mind to make the right decision.
