Crypto Movies and Investments

Knowing the background and current trends in cryptocurrency investments benefits a cryptocurrency enthusiast immensely. Unfortunately, the cryptocurrency industry has advanced tremendously. It’s quite difficult to comprehend it via industry blogs or YouTube videos. The cryptocurrency market is highly volatile. It is possible to make a lot of money within a brief period of time. If you are looking for a crash course on Cryptocurrency, then listed below is a recommendation of the top cryptocurrency investment-related movies for you to watch in 2022.

Movies are one of the most exciting educational channels you can use to learn about cryptocurrency trends. Learning about history, types of coins, future predictions, and how to activate bonuses like the OKX referral bonus code and make money is possible.

Documentary: Bitcoin (The End of Money as We Know it)

Bitcoin – The End Of Money As We Know It

 

Bitcoin: the end of Money as We Know is a crypto movie that reveals the operations of central banks and the finance industries that led to a fiscal crisis. This documentary explores how money and its value trading have advanced and Bitcoin usage.

The thoroughly researched film explains the government’s role in money creation, how the central bank functions, and how its collapse can lead to inflation.

This is a clear and educational crash course about Money investments and Crypto Currencies for anyone who has not yet fully understood the complex Bitcoin topic.

Banking on Bitcoin

Banking on Bitcoin is one of the best and most popular documentaries that offer information coverage on Bitcoin and its connected technologies. Christopher Cannucciari has directed this. It was released in 2016.

The best thing about this film is that it explains every significant development of Cryptocurrency since the origin of Bitcoin in 2008.

The makers of this film focused on different areas, including the rise of Bitcoin. If you want a quick overview of Bitcoin’s historical journey between 2008 and 2016, you should watch this film. It is available to stream on Amazon Prime Video.

Crypto (2019)

Crypto (2019) is one of the most popular cryptocurrency-related films among regular audiences. This is a crime drama-filled thriller film. However, there are many references to crypto technology within the film.

Since the film’s main characters deal with money laundering and other decentralized forms of money, you can see a real-life engraving of digital currency technologies.

Cryptopia: Bitcoin, Blockchains and the Future of the Internet

This crypto movie is the brainchild of Torsten Hoffman, the director of Bitcoin (The End of Money as We Know It). It explains the complexity of the crypto industry, including the good, bad, and ugly truths of the Bitcoin world and how it overcomes those challenges.

Unlike any other crypto movie, Cryptopia gives you essential advice on the top cryptocurrency startup ideas for a long-term investment, especially introducing more crypto assets.

Trust Machine: The Story of Blockchain

Photo Courtesy: David Mcbee, Pexels

Most people talk about bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies in movies nowadays. Despite this, very few know about Blockchain. A blockchain is a decentralized ledger of all transactions across a peer-to-peer network. There is more on Blockchain and its usage.

Blockchain is a fantastic secure technology. It has changed storing of online content. Trust Machine: The Story of Blockchain was created to explain this sophisticated concept.

You can get a complete outlook on the blockchain network, its origin, development, and applications. The film also takes you through the benefits of distributed ledger technology. It also features interview opinions of the experts.

Life on Bitcoin Movie

Cryptocurrencies have wide range of applications, however, it’s not entirely mainstream yet. Especially back in 2014, when it was just six years since Bitcoin came into effect, many investors felt highly troubled to make others understand and accept bitcoin payments.

Life on Bitcoin tells the story of the effort of two young people, Austin and Beccy Craig, to use bitcoin for trading. They accept the challenge of living with nothing but bitcoin for one hundred days. The movie also has a funny outlook as the duo tries to convince people to accept bitcoin payments.

Netflix Explained: Cryptocurrencies (2018)

“Explained” Cryptocurrency (TV Episode 2018)

The streaming service Netflix is now a part of the modern lifestyle, with over a 200 million people using the platform daily. But, of course, it’s also a place where you can learn more about the new technological world through film. For instance, it is one of the best channels you can use to learn about crypto assets and money investments.

In this short cryptocurrency-related film, you can learn about the anonymous and digital currency called ‘cryptocurrency’ and the reason behind its attractions gained from investors.

It explains Cryptocurrency in a 14-minute watch but is a helpful and easy-to-understand episode, how Cryptocurrency works and the introduction to various crypto assets.

Magic Money: The Bitcoin Revolution (2017)

Magic Money is a documentary that explores the mysterious origins of Bitcoin, its role in society, and how Bitcoin can shape the future of our world.

With straightforward narration, this crypto movie is ideal for anyone first dipping their toes into the crypto space.

The Bitcoin Experiment

Governments often questions the legality of Bitcoin, because of its susceptibility to fraud exchange. Despite this, the digital currency tries to break the stereotypes by invading people’s lives and making it into common trading.

The Bitcoin Experiment portrays a social test on bitcoin’s legality. The production crew flew to Scandinavia. They showed it as an example of how the application of digital currency is in a person’s life.

The Blockchain and Us Movie

This movie is not for beginners. Neither is it an introduction to blockchain technology. It does not concentrate on its technical aspects. Instead, it presents a detailed overview of the Blockchain far from hype and starts a conversation about its broader implications in many aspects of our society.

Many people find it boring and hard to sit down and read a book about crypto. Luckily, a wide range of educational movies is easily accessible on almost any streaming platform. Although crypto-related movies are still uncommon, as crypto acceptance continues to grow, there are bound to be more releases soon.
