CryptoComes is a news, analysis and educational platform that covers the crypto industry, Blockchain and new gen tech.

Cyril Gilson is Editor-in-Chief of CryptoComes. In 2015 - 2017 [1], he worked as Editor-in-Chief of Cointelegraph.
