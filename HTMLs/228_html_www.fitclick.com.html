The price of a single stock is only part of the story. You cannot compare the stock prices of two companies without also looking at the total number of shares outstanding to get the market capitalization of the company (market cap). Comparing only stock prices makes no sense. As of this writing, MSFT's share price is $259+-, with about 7.7 billion stocks outstanding, giving a market cap of about $2 trillion. I usually get this kind of information from https://www.checkman.com/quote/msft . This site helps me a lot to analyze stocks of different companies, including Microsoft.
